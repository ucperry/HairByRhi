Welcome to Hair by Rhiannon

For over 20 years, I’ve had the privilege of transforming hair into a true expression of individuality. But for me, styling is about more than just technique—it’s about connection. I find so much joy in getting to know my clients, understanding who they are, and bringing that to life through their hair. For the past four years, my business has operated out of and called Salon Maverick in North Canton its home base, providing a space where creativity and personalization thrive. By blending style, color, and personality, I create looks that not only complement my clients' features but also reflect their essence. My goal is always to ensure that when they leave my chair, they feel confident, seen, and more like themselves than ever before.

The beauty industry has given me the incredible gift of balance—allowing me to be both a dedicated mother to my two children and a passionate stylist. The flexibility of my career has meant that I’ve never had to choose between my love for my craft and my love for my family. Beyond that, it has connected me with so many wonderful families that are as close as right here in North Canton, OH to as far away as Pittsburgh, PA. I’ve had the honor of styling generations of clients, watching children grow up, celebrating life’s big moments, and building lasting relationships. This community isn’t just where I work—it’s where I belong, and I’m grateful every day for the trust and connections that make my job so much more than just a career.

More than just a stylist

One of the ways I like to connect with my clients is by understanding who they are outside of my Chair, so heres a little more about me outside of the salon!

Mom Life

My Heart

Being a mother comes first for me—loving my children fiercely and cherishing every moment I get to be present with them, even when it’s not always easy.

Some days are challenging, but having the chance to watching them grow and become their own little people makes those challenging moments worth it.

Health

Fitness Enthusiast-ish

Staying active—whether it’s through lifting weights, taking classes, trudging through some outdoor cardio or just having fun—is my way of keeping my mind clear and my stress in check. Moving my body not only makes me feel stronger, but it also gives me the mental reset I need to show up as my best self every day.

Music

For anything that ales me

Music is truly my prescription for a better life—it lifts my soul, clears my mind, and makes everything feel a little lighter. I wholeheartedly believe that concerts are the ultimate medicine, where the energy, rhythms, and pure magic of live music give me the freedom to let everything else fade away.

Friends

What I Look Forward Too

My friends are like family—the people who lift me up, make me laugh, and remind me how sweet life can be. No matter how busy life gets, I’m always looking forward to the next get-together, where we pick up right where we left off.
